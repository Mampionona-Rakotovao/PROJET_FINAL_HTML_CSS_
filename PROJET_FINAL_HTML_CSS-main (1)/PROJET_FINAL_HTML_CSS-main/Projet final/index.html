What's about ENI

Mission

Our mission is to train a generation of creative and ethical IT professionals.
... We are committed to contributing sustainable development of Madagascar by offering quality education that tranecends borders

Particularité

The training for obtaining the Professional Bachelor's Degree in computer science was set up at ENI with the three training courses:

  1. Software engineering and database
  2. Administratio of systems and network
  3. General IT
Objectif

Our vision is to be recognized as a center of excellence in computer science, training influential leaders in the global technology industry.
...We aspire to create a dynamic and inclusive learning environment; fostering innovation and contributing to the socio-economic progress of our nation and the world.
